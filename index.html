What Is Hacktiv8ed?

Hacktiv8ed is a next-gen input control ecosystem for e-mobility. We eliminate throttles. We replace sensors. We inject pure digital control directly into EV systems. No noise. No lag. No failure.

The Tech – Injectiv8

The flagship module. A programmable injection unit replacing throttles and pedal assist with a clean digital signal.

  • No moving parts
  • Fully road-legal
  • Plug-and-play with most EV controllers
  • Active in field-tested prototypes
